Our expertise

Earthing, Lightning Protection & EMFI Studies

We provide a broad range of services covering soil analysis, earth grid design, earthing verification, lightning protection, fault current distribution, EMI mitigation and training.

Soil Resistivity Measurement and Analysis

Soil electrical characteristics are the foundation of an effective earthing system. We assess site conditions using proven measurement techniques and accurate soil models.

Methods / Standards

Wenner Method, Schlumberger Method, IEEE 81, AS/NZS practices

Outputs

  • Measured soil resistivity data
  • Uniform, multilayer or multizone soil models
  • Recommendations for optimized grounding design

Fault Current Distribution Analysis

We analyse how fault current flows through the earthing network and surrounding structures to identify high-current zones and improve safety performance.

Focus

Fault pathways, current split, equipment exposure and personnel safety

Outputs

  • Fault current distribution maps
  • Identification of critical high-current areas
  • Design improvement recommendations

New Earth Grid Design

We design earth grids tailored to site conditions, fault levels, soil properties and operational requirements for safe energy dissipation.

Standards

AS/NZS 3000 (where applicable), AS 2067, IEC 61936-1, IEEE 80

Outputs

  • Earth grid layouts and conductor sizing
  • Grid resistance calculations
  • Safe design recommendations for target performance

Existing Earthing System Verification & Modification

We assess existing earthing systems using advanced modelling and site data to identify deficiencies and propose upgrades for compliance and resilience.

Tools

Advanced modelling platforms including XGSLab

Outputs

  • Performance verification reports
  • Compliance gap analysis
  • Upgrade and modification recommendations

EPR, Touch Voltage & Step Voltage Calculations

We perform detailed safety calculations to evaluate voltage gradients during fault conditions and reduce the risk of dangerous touch and step voltages.

Standards

AS 2067, IEEE 80, AS/NZS 3000

Outputs

  • Earth Potential Rise calculations
  • Touch and step voltage assessment
  • Mitigation measures for safer installations

Earth Grid Testing

Field testing verifies that the installed earthing system performs as intended and continues to meet project and standards requirements.

Methods

Fall of Potential Method, Current Injection Test Method

Outputs

  • Measured earth grid resistance
  • Validation of installed system performance
  • Testing and compliance reports

Lightning Protection System Design

We design lightning protection systems to reduce the risk of direct strikes, side flashes and surge-related damage to structures and equipment.

Standards

AS 1768, IEC 62305, NFPA 780 (if applicable)

Outputs

  • Air terminal and down conductor layouts
  • Earthing and bonding arrangements
  • Lightning risk mitigation recommendations

Electromagnetic Interference (EMI / EMFI) Mitigation

We develop mitigation strategies to protect sensitive systems from unwanted electromagnetic interference in industrial and utility environments.

Standards

AS/NZS IEC 61000 series and relevant EMC practices

Outputs

  • Shielding and grounding recommendations
  • Filtering strategies
  • Improved EMC performance for sensitive equipment

Cathodic Protection Design

We provide cathodic protection solutions to help prevent corrosion in pipelines, tanks and other buried or exposed metal structures.

Standards

ISO 15589, NACE standards

Outputs

  • Sacrificial anode system concepts
  • Impressed current system recommendations
  • Long-term corrosion protection strategies

Training and Workshops

We help engineers, technicians and management teams strengthen their understanding of earthing, lightning protection and EMI mitigation through practical training sessions.

Focus Areas

Best practices, standards awareness, testing knowledge and safe design principles

Outputs

  • Tailored workshops for project teams
  • Technical knowledge transfer
  • Practical guidance for real-world applications

Why these studies are crucial

Why Earthing & Lightning Studies Matter

Effective earthing and lightning protection are critical for electrical safety, asset protection and compliance. Poor earthing performance or inadequate lightning protection can increase risk to people, equipment and ongoing operations.

With the right analysis, testing and engineering recommendations, facilities can reduce non-compliance, improve resilience and strengthen long-term system reliability.

  1. 1

    Improve Personnel Safety

    Reduce risks associated with dangerous touch and step voltages under fault conditions.

  2. 2

    Enhance Compliance

    Assess earthing and lightning protection performance against applicable standards and project requirements.

  3. 3

    Protect Critical Assets

    Strengthen protection of substations, process facilities, buildings and infrastructure from electrical fault and lightning-related risks.

  4. 4

    Identify Hidden Weaknesses

    Detect high-resistance and non-compliant earthing points before they become operational or safety issues.

  5. 5

    Support Long-Term Reliability

    Improve maintainability and reliability of electrical infrastructure under challenging site conditions.
