Past Project Experience

High Voltage Safety Management Projects

2025-2026

High Voltage Installation Safety Management Plan

Location: Australia

Development of a comprehensive HV/LV Installation Safety Management Plan including safety rules, operating procedures, permit systems, switching and isolation procedures, maintenance schedules, and operational documentation for safe and compliant electrical system management.

2024-2025

HV Customer Site Documentation - Private HV Network

Location: Mundamia, NSW, Australia

Development of comprehensive documentation for a private internal High Voltage network at a waste management facility in Mundamia. The project included HV protection design coordination, preparation of HVISMP, operating procedures for HV/LV systems, authorised personnel framework, switching instructions, safety documentation, and asset management strategies. The scope also covered liaison for HV metering requirements, safety-in-design documentation, and establishment of maintenance and operational procedures to ensure safe, compliant, and efficient operation of the HV network.

2024-2025

High Voltage Installation Safety Management Plan - 4.99 MW Temora Solar Farm

Location: Temora, NSW

Preparation of a High Voltage Installation Safety Management Plan for a 4.99 MW solar farm project in Temora. The project included development of electrical safety rules, bushfire risk management report, and operation and maintenance templates to support safe and compliant operation of the solar farm’s HV infrastructure. The project focused on establishing structured safety procedures, operational guidelines, and risk management frameworks aligned with utility and regulatory requirements.

2023-2024

High Voltage Installation Safety Management Plan - Flyers Creek Wind Farm

Location: Flyers Creek, NSW

Preparation of a High Voltage Installation Safety Management Plan for the Flyers Creek Wind Farm project. The project included liaison with Essential Energy, review of single line diagrams and ASP Level 3 design, and development of HVISMP in accordance with NSW electrical safety rules and utility requirements. The project ensured safe operational procedures, compliance with regulatory standards, and structured management of HV infrastructure for wind farm operations.
